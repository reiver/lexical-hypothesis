Charles Iliya Krempeaux studied human behavior from the year 2006 to the year 2020. Part of that was trying to understand human (and more generally, animal) personality from a biological and evolutionary point of view. For a time, Charles was a member of the Human Behavior and Evolution Society (HBES). And, from the year 2013 to 2020 was the organizer of the Evolution And Behavior Club.
